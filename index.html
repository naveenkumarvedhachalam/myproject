About Us

Welcome to Scooby Pet and Vet Pharmacy, your trusted neighborhood destination for all things pet care in Virugambakkam, Chennai. Since our inception, we’ve been committed to providing quality products and compassionate veterinary services to pet parents who want the best for their furry, feathered, or scaly family members. At Scooby, we offer a comprehensive range of pet supplies, including premium pet food, grooming essentials, accessories, toys, and more. Our in-house veterinary pharmacy ensures that your pets have access to reliable medications and health products prescribed by qualified professionals. What sets us apart is our personalized care, affordable pricing, and a genuine passion for animals. Whether you’re a first-time pet owner or a seasoned caretaker, our friendly team is here to support you every step of the way. Visit us today and experience a pet shop that truly cares – because at Scooby, your pet's health and happiness come first.
